  • Taeyeon's Musical works

    • In 2004, Taeyeon participated in the 8th SM Youth Selection Contest held by SM Entertainment and won the Best Singer Award and the Grand Prix. As a result, she was officially hired by SM Entertainment and became a trainee of the company. After three years and four months of intensive training, Taeyeon debuted as a member of the girl group Girls' Generation on August 5, 2007.

    On October 7, 2015, she officially debuted as a solo singer and released her first solo album "I". After the release of the album, it ranked first on major music charts in South Korea and won the championship on the iTunes album chart in 13 countries. On October 23, 2015, she held her first solo concert "Taeyeon's Very Special Day".
